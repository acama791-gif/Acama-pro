Welcome to A Click Away Marketing Agency ("ACAMA", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

01

Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

When you fill out a contact form, request a quote, sign up for services, subscribe to newsletters, or contact us, we may collect your full name, email address, phone number, business name, website URL, and marketing goals or project details.

B. Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral sources.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide marketing services, send proposals and invoices, improve our website, analyze marketing performance, and send updates or promotional content if you opt in. We do not sell your personal information.

03

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies, Google Analytics, Meta/Facebook Pixel, and advertising tracking tools to understand user behavior and improve marketing campaigns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

04

How We Share Information

We may share information with trusted service providers such as hosting companies, analytics tools, CRM platforms, advertising platforms, payment processors, or legal authorities if required by law. We ensure third parties handle your data securely.

05

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

06

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations, comply with legal requirements, and resolve disputes.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your country, you may have the right to access your personal data, request correction or deletion, withdraw consent, or opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us below.

08

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

09

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
